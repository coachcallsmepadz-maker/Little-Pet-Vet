Specialized Veterinary Services

We're not your standard cat and dog vet. We offer specialized expertise for the little creatures that matter.

Small Mammal Wellness

Routine check-ups, weight monitoring, and tailored nutritional advice specifically designed for rabbits, guinea pigs, and other small exotic mammals.

Specialized Surgery

Expert surgical procedures including small animal desexing (spay/neuter), delicate mass removals, and specialized abscess treatments.

Advanced Dental Care

Comprehensive burring and dental surgery. Crucial for rabbits and guinea pigs whose teeth never stop growing, ensuring comfort and health.

Dedicated to specialized care, exclusively for small pets.

Giving Back

Home of the Paws Rescue Clinic

Little Pet Vet is proud to be the dedicated home of the Paws Rescue Clinic. We believe every little creature deserves a chance at a healthy, happy life.

We work intimately with local rescue groups like Perth Rescue Angels to provide vital low-cost and free veterinary care for homeless small animals across Perth.

  • Subsidized care for rescues
  • Partnered with Perth Rescue Angels
  • Dedicated rehabilitation support
